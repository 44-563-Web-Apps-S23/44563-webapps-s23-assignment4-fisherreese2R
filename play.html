Fisher Reese snips a play

Tradition by George Middleton

MARY.

Yes. That's right, father; I _had_ to.

OLLIVANT.

[_He stops smoking and looks at her sharply._]

Had to? Who made you?

MARY.

[_Reluctantly._]

It was something inside me.

OLLIVANT.

[_In spite of himself._]

Tush--that foolishness.

MARY.

[_Quickly._]

Don't make it hard for us again.

OLLIVANT.

I made it hard, Mary? Because I objected to you leaving your mother here alone?

MARY.

I remember; you said I was a foolish, "stage-struck" girl.

OLLIVANT.

Well, you're over _that_, aren't you?
