Mary.

hThat's just where you are mistaken, father.

That's why I asked you if you hadn't misunderstood my coming back.

OLLIVANT.

[_Suspiciously._]

Then why did you come at all?

MARY.

I'm human; I wanted to see you and mother, so I came when you generously wrote me. I'm not going to stay and spray the roses.

OLLIVANT.

[_He eyes her tensely and controls himself with an effort._]

So you are not going to stay with your mother and me?
